Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert Drinks

Pico de Gallo

Ingredients

  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno pepper

Directions

Chop tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and jalapeno. Mix in bowl.

Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread

Directions

Toast bread in toaster.

Cereal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry cereal
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

Put cereal in medium sized bowl. Pour milk over cereal.

Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 cups cold water/li>
  • 1 cup sugar/li>
  • ice cubes/li>

Directions

Combine lemon juice and water in a pitcher and add sugar gradually as you stir. Serve with ice cubes in a glass.
