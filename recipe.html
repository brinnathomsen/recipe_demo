Pico de Gallo

Ingredients

  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno pepper

Directions

Chop tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and jalapeno. Mix in bowl.

Cereal
