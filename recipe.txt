Pico de Gallo

Ingredients
4 plum tomatoes
1 medium sized onion
1/2 cup cilantro
1 jalapeno pepper

Directions
Chop tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and jalapeno. Mix in bowl.


Toast

Directions
Toast bread in toaster.


Cereal

Ingredients
1 cup dry cereal
1/2 cup milk

Directions
Put cereal in medium sized bowl. Pour milk over cereal.
